About me

I am a scholar committed to connecting rigorous research with clear, accessible communication. My work explores how emerging technologies and social systems reshape public life, and I am especially interested in the role of institutions in supporting responsible innovation.

Academic focus

My current research examines the interaction between evidence, storytelling, and policy, with projects that bring together archival work, field interviews, and collaborative methods. I value work that is both intellectually ambitious and socially relevant.
